About LetMeExplain

LetMeExplain is a automatic text summarizer that utilizes TextRank, a graph based ranking model for text processing and article summarization.TextRank relies on the semantic relationships between words and phrases to decide relative importance of a sentence within a document.The methodology is in likeness to the way search engines generate relevant web pages from a users search query.
